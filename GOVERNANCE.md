# Governance

## Ownership

OpenResidency is owned and stewarded by **HarmonizedX Limited** (RC 2011004, Abuja,
Nigeria). HarmonizedX holds the copyright on the project's original work and is
responsible for its direction, releases, and its listing as a Digital Public Good.

The software is licensed to everyone under Apache-2.0. Ownership of the project (the
name, the roadmap stewardship, the release process) is distinct from the rights the
license grants: anyone may use, modify, and redistribute the code, including for
commercial and government deployments, without permission.

## Maintainers

The maintainer team is appointed by HarmonizedX. Maintainers review and merge changes,
cut releases, and triage security reports.

- Lead maintainer: HarmonizedX Limited (engineering)
- Add individual maintainers here as the community grows.

## How decisions are made

- Routine changes (fixes, adapters, docs) are made by pull request and require review
  and approval from at least one maintainer.
- Substantial changes (new standards, breaking API changes, changes to the privacy or
  security model) require a short written proposal in an issue and maintainer consensus.
- HarmonizedX retains final decision authority as the project owner, exercised sparingly
  and in the open.
